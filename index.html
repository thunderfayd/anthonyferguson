Virtual Receptionist+

Your phone gets answered 24/7 — even during the dinner rush, after hours, and on holidays. Our virtual receptionist sounds natural, knows your menu, hours, and specials, handles reservations, and texts you when something needs your attention.

What you get: Never miss a call again. No more voicemail. No more lost customers. For less than the cost of one shift per week.

How It Works↓
01A customer calls your business. If you’re busy, closed, or just can’t pick up — the call forwards automatically to your virtual receptionist.
02The receptionist answers with a friendly, natural voice — using your business name, and already trained on your menu, hours, specials, and FAQs.
03It handles the call — answers questions, takes reservation details, or provides directions. No hold music, no voicemail, no lost customers.
04You get a text with a full summary of the call — who called, what they needed, and any action required. You stay in control without being tied to the phone.
